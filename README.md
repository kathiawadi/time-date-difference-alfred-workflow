# time-date-difference-alfred-workflow
Time &amp; Date Calculator is an Alfred workflow that lets you do natural-language time and date arithmetic directly from the Alfred search bar — no apps, no switching context.

Just use the letter t and hit a space after that to trigger the workflow. 

What you can calculate:

Time difference — 12:30 pm - 7:00 am → 5 hours 30 minutes (also shows decimal hours and total minutes)
Time addition — 9:00 am + 2h 30m → 11:30 AM (also shows 24-hour format)
Decimal hour subtraction — 7.6hrs - 3h → 4 hours 36 minutes
Date difference — 25/03/2026 - 15/04/1990 → 35 years, 11 months, 10 days (plus total days, hours, minutes)
Date + time difference — 25/03/2026 9:00am - 15/04/1990 5:30pm → full breakdown including time-of-day remainder
Single date lookup — type any date to see how far it is from today (e.g. 2 months, 14 days from now)
Single time — converts between 12-hour and 24-hour formats on the fly



Date formats accepted: DD/MM/YYYY, YYYY-MM-DD, 25 Mar 2026, 25-Mar-26, plus keywords like today, tomorrow, yesterday.

Results are copy-ready — pressing ↵ on any result copies it to your clipboard instantly.Sonnet 4.6
